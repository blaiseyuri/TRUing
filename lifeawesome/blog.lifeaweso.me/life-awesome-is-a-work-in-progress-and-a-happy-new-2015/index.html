Life Awesome Is A Work In Progress (and a Happy New 2015)

published January 12, 2015

by lifeaweso.me

What's your new year's resolution?
2015 is already here. We hope you had a great time. Actually we hope you had an awesome time.
Why awesome?
Because you're awesome.
This is the main point of lifeaweso.me.

As promised, we're trying to keep you updated about our work in progress. There were some changes in the last weeks, technical and non-technical decisions, along with some other more boring stuff.

In order to let you have a taste of lifeaweso.me ASAP, we've decided to try and release a closed Alpha for all those who will be still subscribed to our newsletter at the time being. We're talking about February 2015 (and we'll do our best and try to respect that schedule). We will be releasing more details about it very soon so stay tuned. The closed Alpha will have huge limitations, and obviously there will be a lot of things not yet developed, not working properly and a lot of bugs.
We'll need you and your feedback in order to release a more stable Beta version in the near future, so just have a little more patience and we'll send you all the details in one of the next emails.

the lifeaweso.me crew
