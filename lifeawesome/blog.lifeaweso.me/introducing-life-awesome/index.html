Introducing Life Awesome

published November 29, 2014

by lifeaweso.me

Here we are.
A month ago lifeaweso.me was barely an idea.
Today we are releasing our new website and our new blog (thanks to Ghost) into the wild.

It's pretty scary - and incredibly exciting. Myself and Daniele are working very hard and we're still at the beginning, but we planned so much more, and it's really complicated to write about all the crazy stuff we came up to without becoming boring. I tried to keep it simple, but if this post seems still too long, skip directly to our tl;dr section.

We're not releasing a lot of information about Life Awesome yet, but this blog has the purpose of keeping you updated about our progress in this incredible journey.

And here we are, creating this amazing opportunity to talk a little bit more about the purpose and the value of our idea. We'll be talking about technology and design further on.

Without purpose we would not exist.

What is Life Awesome?

First of all, the answer is not 42.
This is more of a personal question, trust me.
We have Facebook. And then we have LinkedIn. And again, we have online portfolio platforms, or private diaries apps, or even travel organizers.
As a huge sport & activity fan, I enjoy doing all kind of crazy stuff, like snowboarding, diving, parachuting, rafting. I use my GoPro a lot, and I've been through all those awesome experiences that shaped me the way I am. Yes, I have social networks to share these accomplishments with my friends, but it feels like everything I achieve, a new personal record, a new project (like Life Awesome) being born, fades away in this data pollution we have today.
Facebook (and no, I don't have an account there), has this funny and entertaining timeline (which is our main refactoring project in Life Awesome) to click back and relive a memory (which is usually you getting drunk with your friends on Saturday night, not the achievement we're looking to push here). And because their timeline catalogues everything, it makes impossible for the real you to have the right space that deserves.

We believe people deserve a simple and efficient way of creating a personal lifetime achievements book. Whether you are an accomplished businessman or simply a hitchhiker wanting to keep and track all of your movements across the globe. We're building Life Awesome around all those people that have accomplished great things in their lives.

Because we believe people are awesome.

Do not go where the path may lead, go instead where there is no path and leave a trail.

TL;DR

Are you a sport & activity person, an artist, a businessman? Do you travel a lot? Are you a part of a rock band (or maybe just a DJ performing in different clubs every weekend)?
Life Awesome is tailored for people like you.
Life Awesome will offer you a completely new approach to a social network.
Life Awesome will give you the focus and simplicity stretched in a minimal timeline that will allow you to show others the real you and what are you capable of.
